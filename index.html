How to Play

Game Setup

  1. Choose grid size: 3√ó3, 4√ó4, or 5√ó5.
  2. Select AI difficulty (Easy, Medium, or Hard) for PvC mode.
  3. Pick game mode: Player vs Player (PvP) or Player vs Computer (PvC).
  4. Choose your favorite theme color.
  5. Select unique emojis (Defaults: X/O).

Gameplay

  1. Players take turns placing their emoji on an empty cell.
  2. Get N identical emojis in a row (N=grid size) to win.
  3. Winning line can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.
  4. Line must contain *only* the winner's emoji.
  5. If board is full with no winner, it's a draw.

Buttons

  • New Game: Reset scores & start fresh.
  • Restart: New round, keep scores.
  • : Share game link.
  • /: Toggle theme.
  • /: Toggle sound.
